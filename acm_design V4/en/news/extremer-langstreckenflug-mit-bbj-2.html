Extreme long distance flight with BBJ 2

December 9, 2014

A BBJ 2 (Boeing Business Jet), which was equipped by Lufthansa Technik until the beginning of 2014 and then delivered, recently completed an extremely long-haul flight from Rio de Janeiro in Brazil to Frankfurt am Main.

Aircraft equipped by Lufthansa Technik

The aircraft, operated by the company ACM AIR CHARTER for a private customer, flew the distance of 9,524 kilometers (5,139 nautical miles) with six passengers on board and required a flight time of 12:02 hours.

Thomas Minninger, Managing Director of ACM AIR CHARTER, said:

We are really impressed with the performance of the BBJ 2. The range of the aircraft and the high-quality interior, which guarantees passengers well-being, especially on long-haul flights, prove that the equipment experts at Lufthansa Technik have done an excellent job. Flying on this aircraft is pure pleasure.

The ultra-modern cabin design of the BBJ 2 was created by a renowned London design firm. The aircraft has been optimized for long-haul operations through the use of lightweight materials and consistent design, for example by taking into account the ideal center of gravity.

Despite the focus of design and engineering on weight reduction and range, the BBJ's cabin incorporates various innovative technologies. These include, among other things, a steam shower, an open cooking facility and an air humidification system.

In addition, the aircraft is equipped with Lufthansa Technik's state-of-the-art nice® in-flight entertainment and cabin management system, including GSM connectivity and live TV. The nice® system can be fully controlled by personal electronic devices such as iPad or iPhone. In order to create a real cinema feeling, the BBJ 2 was the first Boeing 737 to be equipped with a 65-inch monitor (165 cm screen diagonal) in HD resolution.
